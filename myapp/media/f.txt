What is health?
Answer: Health is a state of complete well-being, both physical and mental, and is not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. It is influenced by factors such as nutrition, exercise, sleep, and stress management.
What is preventive medicine?
Answer: Preventive medicine involves taking measures to prevent diseases rather than treating them. This includes practices such as vaccinations, regular medical checkups, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
What are the symptoms of diabetes?
Answer: Symptoms of diabetes may include excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision, and slow healing of wounds.
What to do in case of a minor burn?
Answer: In the case of a minor burn, it is advised to cool the affected area under cold water for at least 10 minutes, cover the burn with a clean bandage, and consult a doctor if necessary.
What is a heart attack and what are its symptoms?
Answer: A heart attack occurs when part of the heart does not receive enough blood. Symptoms may include intense chest pain, pain in the arms, jaw, or back, cold sweats, and difficulty breathing.
